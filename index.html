About RafApp

If you have a hard time finding the ideal application to keep track of your collections, you are at the right place.

Most of the applications available in the wide variety of operating systems are limiting users in their usage of the application. Users are forced to pick one of the predetermined types of items and their predetermined attributes. Unlike other applications, RafApp should allow users to create their own types of items and keep track of them. Additionally, users should be able to name attributes for each type of the item created and define attributes types as well.
